# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how failfa.st ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar
technologies to recognize, collect, and store information when you visit our website, use our
services, or interact with our content. This policy is designed to help you understand what cookies
are, why we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or device by websites that you visit.
They are widely used to make websites work, or to improve the efficiency of a website, as well as to
provide information to the website's owners. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.
Persistent cookies remain on your device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as
soon as you close your web browser.

## How we use cookies

We use cookies for several purposes, including:

1. **Functional cookies**: These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of our website and
   services. They enable features such as user authentication, remembering your user settings, and
   ensuring a secure user experience. Without these cookies, some parts of our website may not
   function properly.

2. **Third-party cookies**: We use third-party services to enhance your experience on our website,
   and these services may set cookies on your device. Examples of these services include embedded
   YouTube videos and CodeSandbox embeds. These cookies are subject to the privacy policies of the
   respective third-party service providers.

## Functional cookies we use

Here is a list of the main functional cookies we use, and a brief description of their purpose:

-   next-auth: This cookie is used to manage user authentication and maintain user sessions. It
    enables features such as remembering your login status and providing a secure user experience.

-   user_settings: This cookie is used to remember your preferences and settings on our website,
    such as your preferred language or display options.

## Third-party cookies we use

Here is a list of the main third-party cookies we use, and a brief description of their purpose:

-   YouTube: We embed YouTube videos on our website, which may result in YouTube setting cookies on
    your device. YouTube uses these cookies to track your interactions with the videos and for other
    purposes as described in their privacy policy, which can be found at
    [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

-   CodeSandbox: We use CodeSandbox embeds to provide interactive coding examples on our website.
    CodeSandbox may set cookies on your device for purposes such as tracking your interactions with
    the embedded content. For more information about CodeSandbox's privacy practices, please refer
    to their privacy policy at
    [https://codesandbox.io/legal/privacy](https://codesandbox.io/legal/privacy).

## Your choices regarding cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please keep in mind that removing or blocking
cookies may impact your user experience and some features may not function properly.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. To learn more
about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them,
visit [www.aboutcookies.org](https://www.aboutcookies.org) or
[www.allaboutcookies.org](https://www.allaboutcookies.org).
